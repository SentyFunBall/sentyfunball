SenTOS - A Discord Bot

What is SenTOS?

SenTOS is a Discord bot made by my stupid ass. It is currently in development, and is not yet available for public use, however is in a handful of servers for testing purposes.

Features

  • An advanced Score system for users to track their activity, optionally punishing bad behavior

  • Fun stuff like polls and a leaderboard

  • An AI chatbot powered by OpenAI's GPT models

Development

SenTOS is being developed using JavaScript and the Discord.js library. It is hosted on SentyTek's private servers.

Privacy Notice

SenTOS (Referred to as "The Bot" or "Bot"), while using the AI chatbot, processes Discord messages and public facing user status information to generate responses using OpenAI's API.

We do not store or share this information with third parties, nor build profiles on our users.

Data is transmitted to OpenAI for message processing and is subject to their Privacy Policy.

By using the Bot, you agree to this Privacy Notice and consent to the processing of your data as described herein.

You may request confirmation that no data is retained at any time by contacting SentyFunball on Discord.
